Laukifossen

Laukifossen is a beautiful waterfall that is also used as a power plant

About the waterfall

Laukifossen is a small but beautiful waterfall. The meltwater from the glacier provides a beautiful palette of colors in the water, with shades from deep blue to copper green. The surrounding trees and mountains, as well as the old buildings and wooden bridge make this one of the most beautiful places in the village.

The power plant

Since 1917 there has been a power plant in this waterfall. The annual production is 200 megawatt hours. To put this into context, this is enough power to:

  • Power 10 households for a year
  • Charge 1.3 million phones
  • Charge 40 million toothbrushes
  • Watch TV for 228 years
  • Drive an electric car 30 times around the world

Information retrieved from NVE.
