Skarsteinsetra

The hike to Skarsteinsetra starts from the center of Olden. Municipal road to Fagerli. From there, you take the Forest Road to Skarsteinsetra. The hike up takes about 30-40 minutes, and is on both a forest road and a nice trail. It is possible to push a stroller up.

At Skarsteinsetra there is a nice view over the fjord and towards Loen and Rake/Oppheim.
