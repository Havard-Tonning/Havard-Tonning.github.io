Sundekiosken

Sundekiosken is perfect if you want a quick bite to eat. Here they have the dishes you typically get in a fast food restaurant, such as burgers, fries and hot dogs. In addition to this, they have dishes from from Asian cuisine, such as wok, noodles and spring rolls.

You can find Sundekiosken in the gas station by the cruise dock.
