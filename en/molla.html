Mølla Gjestehus

Mølla ("The Mill") has seafood, meat, pizza and burgers. Here, you can get served fish that only few hours earlier swam in the clear watersof the fjord. The restaurant has seating both inside and out on the terrace.

Mølla also has a bar if you want something to drink to the food.

It is not difficult to find Mølla. The restaurant is located in the big white building by the port.
