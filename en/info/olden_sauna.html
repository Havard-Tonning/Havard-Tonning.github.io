Olden Sauna

The Olden Sauna is located right next to Oldevatn camping, an area known for its spectacular nature, including the famous Briksdalsbreen glacier. This is the perfect place to relax after a day of adventures in the area.

  • Electric stove with preheating
  • Changing room
  • Water (freshwater)
  • 6-8 people
  • Free WIFI
  • Panoramic window
  • Beach
