Olden Apartments

Olden apartments 1 is located in Olden. This apartment features a garden, barbecue facilities, free WiFi and free private parking.

This apartment with mountain and river views has 2 bedrooms and a patio. Opening onto a terrace with sea views, this apartment also features a satellite flat-screen TV, a well-equipped kitchen with a fridge, a dishwasher and an oven, as well as 1 bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

Skiing is possible in the surrounding area, and Olden apartments 1 also offers ski storage space.

Sandane Airport, Anda is 62 km from the property.
