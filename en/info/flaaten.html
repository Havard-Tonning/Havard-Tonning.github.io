Flåten Feriehytter

6 cozy holiday cottages with panoramic views of majestic mountains, glaciers, clear water and green valleys. The cottages are located in a peaceful area and are partially shielded from each other. Good hiking opportunities in the beautiful nature. The kitchen is well equipped with everything you need for tableware and cooking, as well as a dishwasher. There are two bedrooms that sleep 4-6, shower and toilet in all cottages.
