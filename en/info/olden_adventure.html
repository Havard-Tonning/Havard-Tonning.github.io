Olden Adventure

Olden Cruise is a local transport company established in 2005.

We offer a range of vehicles with capacities from 8 to 78 seats, and we would love to show you our beautiful countryside.

Our most popular tour is the Briksdal Shuttle Bus service. From May 2017 we also offer a shuttle bus to the brand new and spectacular Loen Skylift.

We also offer open-top sightseeing to the glacier, private tours and transport.
