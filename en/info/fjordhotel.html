Olden Fjordhotel

Olden Fjordhotel is a classic and charming hotel beautifully situated by the Nordfjord. Here you stay comfortably by the fjord, in rooms with balconies and views of the mighty fjord landscape. The hotel has a restaurant with panoramic views, a nostalgic bar and a historic disco that gives your stay a very special atmosphere.

With Olden Fjordhotel as a base you can easily explore the nature and experiences in the region. After a day of hiking, the hotel is a perfect place to return to - for good food, relaxing surroundings and views of the fjord.
