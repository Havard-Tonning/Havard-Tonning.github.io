Stabukk

Stabukk is a café that serves waffles, coffee, ice cream, soft ice cream, buns and shakes. The café has outdoor seating where you can enjoy the summer sun. Stabukk is staffed by many bright and happy local young people, ready to welcome you with a big smile and an ice cream even bigger.

Stabukk is located right next to Bunnpris in the centre of Olden.
