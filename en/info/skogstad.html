Skogstad Sport

At Skogstad you will find high-quality sports and outdoor clothing for the whole family. We have a strong focus on the environment, design and functionality at reasonable prices.

Our store in Olden offers the season's collections with a wide selection for both children, women and men. For those who need technical quality garments adapted to different activities and weather conditions, regardless of whether it is for seasonal mountain hikes or for urban city life.

Olden also has its own outlet store that is open during the summer. Here you will find discontinued models and colors from previous collections, at greatly reduced prices.
