Olden Active

Olden Active is owned and operated by father and son Briksdal, and is a total provider of experiences in the inner Nordfjord. With over 30 years of experience in the tourism industry - and a family tradition that stretches back more than 150 years - we facilitate professional and memorable experiences in spectacular western Norway nature.

We offer rental and guided experiences in and around Olden, where fjords, mountains and glaciers create a unique setting for active nature experiences. As a local player, we are committed to sustainable development, local value creation and genuine hospitality. Our guides and skippers are certified according to current industry standards and ensure safe, personal and professional experiences.

For generations, we have welcomed guests from all over the world to Olden and facilitated experiences close to nature and in harmony with the surroundings. Through activities such as RIB tours, kayaking, SUP and e-biking, we want to give our guests unforgettable experiences in the heart of the best Nordfjord has to offer.
