The Viking Shop

Vikingbutikken is a souvenir shop in the center of Olden. In the shop you will find Viking items, such as drinking horns, beer mugs, Viking jewelry etc. We focus on sustainability, so here you will find quality Norwegian products. We can mention Norlender knitted jumpers, knives from Helle, silver jewelery from Fjordsylv, bronze jewelery from Espeland, leather bags from Aurlands and reindeer, sheep and goat skins from Granberg.

Welcome to our small Viking shop with a local twist.
