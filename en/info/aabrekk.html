Aabrekk gard Trollbu

Aabrekk Gard Trollbu is located at the top of the beautiful Oldedalen in Stryn municipality, we offer accommodation and have a café/dining room for parties and groups.

The farm and Trollbutunet are located in a quiet area, at the foot of the Brenndalsbreen glacier, and it is only a few minutes' drive from us to the famous Briksdalsbreen glacier. On the way here you also drive past the beautiful turquoise Oldevatnet, which is worth a visit in itself. On the way to Brenndalen you can meet our sheep that graze there in the summer.

We offer accommodation in a cabin and 2 restored houses from the 18th and 19th centuries, in Trollbutunet. We rent out cabins and apartments from May to the end of September. Search for "Trollbu Aabrekk gard" on booking.com to book.

Up in the yard on the farm we have a dining room with plenty of space for parties and groups, where we serve homemade food. It also has an open café in the main season.

In the café we serve homemade farm food and cakes, and sell food produced on the farm, as well as home-brewed beer. We also sell ice cream from Diplom is.

The products delivered from the farm are based on strong traditions, and are called Lykle.

The opening hours of the café can be found on our website www.aabrekkgard.no, our Google account for “Aabrekk Gard Kafé og restaurant” or on “Aabrekk gard” on Facebook.
