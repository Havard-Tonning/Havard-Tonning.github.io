Ljós

A small independent gift shop by the fjord in Olden, offering carefully curated Norwegian design and craftsmanship.

We focus on quality, authenticity, and locally rooted aesthetics — from our own hand-poured LJÓS soy candles and essential-oil blends to fine-art photography, ceramics, woodwork, linocuts, illustrations, handmade cookies, and more.

Most items are created by us or by skilled Norwegian artists and producers we collaborate with closely. We also carry a small selection of thoughtfully chosen Swedish brands.

📍 Open most summer days 10:00-17:00, depending on the cruise ship schedule. Opening hours are updated weekly.

Visit us by the fjord and discover something meaningful — genuinely Scandinavian.
