Riccovero

The tailoring heritage of Riccovero dates back to 1936. Surrounded by the deep fjords and high mountains of Western Norway, Einar Kvamme started his tailoring practice in Olden. Since then, the tailoring heritage has developed through 3 generations.

In 1986, grandson Finn Einar Kvamme established the RICCOVERO brand, taking the family business to a whole new dimension. The craftsmanship was combined with exclusive textiles from Italy's most renowned weavers. With an eye for detail, a strong focus on tailoring and fit, and the use of quality fabrics, the identity of Riccovero was created.

We are constantly working to develop a brand that inspires an urban and individual style. The tailoring heritage will always be the cornerstone of the company, and the pride that defines our concept.
