Vanberg Hytter

Vanberg Hytter has been operating with tourism, cabins and rentals as an additional business on the farm since the late 60s. Today we have three cabins, one house and an apartment that we rent out. The cabins are summer cabins that can only be rented in the summer months. The apartment and the house can be rented for most of the year. Everything is sheltered with its own outdoor area and a good distance from neighboring houses.
