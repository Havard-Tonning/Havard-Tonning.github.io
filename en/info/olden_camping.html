Olden Camping

Plassen is surrounded by high mountains, waterfalls, water and glaciers, and has a spectacular view of the glaciers, with good fishing opportunities, with free boat rentals for our guests. There is also a lovely wood-fired sauna, with a fantastic view into the valley.

You will find us 13.5 km from the center of Olden, on Fv 5724 Olden - Briksdalsbreen. We are the second campsite after the pipe tunnel, NB! Look for Elgen at the entrance.

Oldedalsvegen 1296, 6788 OLDEN

Olden Camping is a good starting point for hiking in the mountains, forests and fields, guided glacier tours or day trips by car.

The hosts at Olden Camping place great emphasis on service and cleanliness! Fresh rolls or bread every day after ordering the evening before. Open in the summer from 1 May to 15 September.
