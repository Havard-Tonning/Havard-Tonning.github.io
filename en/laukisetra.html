Laukisetra

There are four farmers who each have their own farm. The barns are no longer standing. When they had to take the cows to the pastures earlier in the year, they brought the cattle on a long detour thorugh Eidemaka. The pasture ridge was too steep for the cows. At Laukisetra they used to have a zip-line for transporting the milk to the farm.

Follow the signs to Laukisetra through the farmyard to the sawmill. There is a nice forest road to Laukisetra.
