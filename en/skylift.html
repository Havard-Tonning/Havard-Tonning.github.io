Loen Skylift

Loen Skylift is one of the steepest cable cars in the world and lifts you a thousand meters almost straight up - to the restaurant on the edge of the mountain Hoven. Up here you come into close contact with the mighty nature. Whether you sit inside the comfortable amphitheater in the restaurant, stand on the roof or walk in the mountain world up here, you will get a nature experience on the edge of what you thought was possible. Norway's easiest summit hike.
