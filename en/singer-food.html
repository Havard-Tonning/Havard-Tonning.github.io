Singerheimen

Singerheimen is the former home of multi-millionaire William Singer and his wife Anna Singer. Today the home is used for both accommodation and dining, and Wednesday to Sunday there is afternoon tea at 1:00 PM and 3:00 PM. Here, you have the opportunity to travel back in time in the main house at Singerheimen, where everything has been preserved in the authentic style from the mid-20th century.

You are served rolls with local cured ham and smoked salmon, scones with delicious raspberry jam, and sweet cakes. And of course tea (or coffee). All of the bread and cakes are made in house, and the ingredients are procured locally. The food is served on 100-year-old tableware, which reflects the way Singerheimen is run - preservation through use.

You will find Singerheimen up in the hills just outside the village center. The large green houses are hard to miss.
